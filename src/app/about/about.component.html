About Dfam

Dfam is a database of repetitive DNA elements organized around multiple sequence alignments of TE families. The seed alignment provides a model-neutral representation of a sequence family from which both a traditional consensus seqeunce and a profile hidden Markov model (HMM) may be built. The early releases of Dfam used RepeatMasker and the TE database, Repbase to bootstrap the generation of these seed alignments.
